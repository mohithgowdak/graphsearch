# Password & Account FAQ

## How do I reset my password?

Go to **Settings > Security > Reset Password** and enter the email address
associated with your account. You will receive a reset link within a few
minutes. The link expires after 24 hours.

## How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Open **Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication** and scan the QR code
with an authenticator app such as Authy or Google Authenticator. Save the
backup codes somewhere safe — they are shown only once.

## My account is locked. What do I do?

Accounts lock automatically after five failed sign-in attempts. Wait 15
minutes and try again, or contact support at help@example.com to unlock it
immediately.
