# Returns & Refunds Policy

Items can be returned within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund,
provided they are unused and in their original packaging.

To start a return, visit the **Orders** page, select the item, and click
"Return item". A prepaid shipping label will be emailed to you.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days
of the returned item arriving at our warehouse.

## Exceptions

- Perishable goods and gift cards are not returnable.
- Sale items marked "final sale" cannot be returned.
- Damaged or defective items can be returned at any time; contact
  help@example.com with a photo of the damage.
