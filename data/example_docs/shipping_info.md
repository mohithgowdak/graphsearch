# Shipping Information

## Delivery options

- **Standard shipping** (free over $50): 3–5 business days.
- **Express shipping** ($9.99): 1–2 business days.
- **Same-day delivery**: available in select metro areas for orders placed
  before 12:00 local time.

## Order tracking

Once your order ships you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can
also find tracking numbers on the **Orders** page of your account.

## International shipping

We currently ship to the US, Canada, the EU, the UK, Japan, and Australia.
Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and
are calculated at checkout where supported.
